Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-14

RepDeck by Mindrix Labs is a fitness planning app. We designed RepDeck to work without a user account or a backend. Your plans and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data We Process

  • Custom plans and exercises you create
  • App preferences (e.g., filters, settings, reminders)

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Permissions

On Android 13+, RepDeck asks for the Notifications permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) to send reminders that you enable. If you deny permission, reminders are disabled.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Security

Because data is local to your device, please use device-level protections such as a passcode or biometrics. RepDeck does not transmit your data to our servers.

Children’s Privacy

RepDeck is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app if you are under the minimum age required by your local laws without parental consent.

Changes

We may update this policy over time. We will change the “Last updated” date when we do.
