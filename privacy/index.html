RepDeck – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-14

1. Overview

RepDeck is a fitness planning app designed to work entirely offline. We don’t require accounts, and we don’t store your data on our servers. Everything stays on your device.

2. Data We Store

All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Custom workout plans and exercises you create
  • App settings and preferences (filters, reminders, etc.)

If you uninstall RepDeck, this data is removed from your device.

3. Permissions

Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): On Android 13+, we may request permission to send reminders you enable in the app. If you deny this permission, reminders will be disabled.

We do not request any other sensitive permissions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or send your data to anyone.

5. Security

Since all data is stored on your device, please protect it with your device’s security features (e.g., passcode, fingerprint, or face unlock).

6. Children’s Privacy

RepDeck is not intended for children under 13. If you are under the minimum age required by local law, only use the app with a parent or guardian’s consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date above.
